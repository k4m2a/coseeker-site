Help

We're a small team and we read every message.

Help Guidelines Privacy Terms

However we can help — a question about your account, a problem with the app, a worry about something you've seen, or just feedback — the best way to reach us is by email.

Email us at contact@coseeker.org and we'll get back to you.

A few things that help us help you faster:

  • Tell us what you were trying to do, and what happened instead.
  • Include the handle or email associated with your CoSeeker account.
  • If something looks wrong on screen, a screenshot goes a long way.

Reporting something on the platform

If you've come across a post or profile that breaks our Community Guidelines, you can report it directly — there's a report option on every post and profile. For anything more sensitive, or that the in-app report doesn't cover, write to us at contact@coseeker.org.

Security issues

If you've found a security problem, please tell us at contact@coseeker.org rather than exploiting it. We're grateful for responsible disclosure.

Got questions?
Here are the answers.

CoSeeker is an invite-only social space for sincere seekers — people exploring life, consciousness and existence. It's a place to ask deeper questions, share direct observations, and find others who care about understanding rather than winning.
Anyone genuinely exploring the questions that shape a life — across traditions, practices and backgrounds. CoSeeker is for people who want patience and honesty over performance and outrage.
CoSeeker grows carefully through invitations. Request one and tell us a little about what you're exploring — we read every request personally and reach out as invitations open. If you already have an invite, you can enter CoSeeker to get started.
No targeted advertising, no follower hierarchy, no engagement traps, and no feed engineered for endless scrolling. Every product choice is measured against one principle: does it move a person toward sincere inquiry, direct observation, or meaningful connection?
CoSeeker is built on the open AT Protocol. That means your words remain yours and your social connections can move across a wider network — your identity and relationships aren't locked inside one company.
No. CoSeeker is not a guru marketplace, a follower-building platform, or a stage for performing certainty. Openness here must never be traded for money, influence, access, or devotion.
CoSeeker is one of the first public tools from K4M2A Foundation, a larger effort to build technology that supports human maturity, harmony, and awakening. You can read more in our Manifesto.
We'd love to hear it. Reach the team through the Contact page or email support@coseeker.com.
